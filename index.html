CORDIALLY INVITES YOU TO CELEBRATE HIS BIRTHDAY

WHY IS THIS HAPPENING NOW?

In an ideal world, birthdays would be celebrated on time.
Unfortunately, we do not live in an ideal world.

We live in a world where:

  • international law is more of a suggestion,
  • calendars are aspirational,
  • and personal milestones must occasionally yield to geopolitical chaos.

Thus, in the spirit of resilience, delayed gratification, and mildly unhinged optimism,
we proceed.

Think of this less as a birthday
and more as the post-credits scene for an awfully mediocre trilogy.

DRESS CODE

(Very Chill, Promise)

Elevated Casual / Soft Formal / “I Tried (and That’s Enough)”

This is not a black-tie event.
It is also not a “grabbed the first thing off the chair” event.
Somewhere comfortably in between.

The general vibe is:

  • Avengers grabbing shawarma after saving the city
  • Everyone looks unintentionally well-dressed
  • Everyone admits they thought about it
  • Nobody thought about it

A few gentle suggestions (not rules, no policing):

  • Comfort is the most important factor, effort is appreciated
  • If you spent a few minutes deciding what to wear, perfect
  • If you didn’t, that’s also completely fine

No pressure to outshine anyone.
Just show up looking like you cared a little that’s genuinely it.

If you’re unsure:
Dress one notch nicer than your usual lunch outfit and you’ll be more than okay.

comfortable chaotic in a controlled way and significantly more stylish than it has any right to be
